## üñºÔ∏è **IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS**

### **Recommended Sizes**:
- **Service Cards**: 800x600px (4:3 aspect ratio)
- **Testimonial Photos**: 400x400px (square, 1:1)
- **Calculator Background**: 1920x1080px (16:9)
- **About Section**: 1200x800px (3:2)
- **Certifications**: 600x400px (3:2)
- **Hero Background**: 1920x1080px (16:9)
- **Portfolio Items**: 1200x900px (4:3)

### **File Format**:
- **JPG/JPEG** for photos (smaller file size)
- **PNG** for logos/certifications with transparency
- **WebP** for modern browsers (optional, better compression)

### **File Naming Convention**:
Use descriptive, lowercase names with hyphens:
- ‚úÖ `service-granite-chippings.jpg`
- ‚úÖ `testimonial-client-1.jpg`
- ‚úÖ `calculator-background.jpg`
- ‚ùå `IMG_1234.jpg` (not descriptive)
- ‚ùå `Service Image.jpg` (spaces and capitals)

## üé® **IMAGE STYLE GUIDELINES**

### **Colors**:
- Match your brand (blue tones)
- Natural lighting preferred
- Avoid overly saturated colors

### **Composition**:
- Clear focus on products
- Professional appearance
- Show scale (people/equipment for reference)

### **Quality**:
- High resolution (at least 1200px width)
- Sharp and in focus
- Good contrast
