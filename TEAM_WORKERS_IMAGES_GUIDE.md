## ğŸ“¸ **PHOTOGRAPHY TIPS**

### **For Owner/CEO Photo**:
- **Location**: Office, facility, or professional studio
- **Attire**: Business professional (suit, or company uniform)
- **Pose**: Confident, approachable
- **Background**: Office, facility, or neutral
- **Lighting**: Natural or professional studio lighting
- **Expression**: Friendly, professional smile

### **For Team Member Photos**:
- **Consistency**: Same background/lighting for all
- **Attire**: Business casual or company uniform
- **Style**: Professional but approachable
- **Group or Individual**: Either works

### **For Worker Photos**:
- **Safety First**: Show workers with proper safety gear (hard hats, vests)
- **Action Shots**: Workers actively working, not posing
- **Equipment**: Show them operating machinery
- **Natural**: Candid shots work best
- **Variety**: Different angles, different operations

### **For Process Photos**:
- **Clear Focus**: Show the specific process clearly
- **Workers Visible**: People should be in the photos
- **Equipment**: Show relevant equipment/tools
- **Action**: Workers actively doing the task
