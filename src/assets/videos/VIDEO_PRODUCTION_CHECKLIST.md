# Video Production Checklist for DonRock Promotional Video

## Quick Start Guide

Since you need a promotional video, here are your options:

### Option 1: Hire a Professional (Recommended)
**Best for**: High-quality, professional results

**Steps:**
1. Find a local videographer in Lagos/Nigeria
2. Share the script from `PROMOTIONAL_VIDEO_SCRIPT.md`
3. Provide access to your facility/products
4. Review and approve final video
5. Receive final MP4 file

**Where to find:**
- Google: "video production Lagos Nigeria"
- Fiverr: Search "promotional video Nigeria"
- Upwork: Search "video editor Nigeria"
- Local marketing agencies

**Budget**: ₦50,000 - ₦200,000+ (varies by quality)

---

### Option 2: Use AI Video Generator (Fastest)
**Best for**: Quick creation without filming

**Tools:**
1. **Synthesia** (synthesia.io)
   - AI avatars and voiceover
   - Professional templates
   - ~$30-50/month

2. **InVideo AI** (invideo.io)
   - AI video generation
   - Stock footage integration
   - ~$15-30/month

3. **Pictory** (pictory.ai)
   - Text-to-video AI
   - Auto-editing
   - ~$19-47/month

**Steps:**
1. Sign up for service
2. Use the script from `PROMOTIONAL_VIDEO_SCRIPT.md`
3. Select template/style
4. Add your logo and text
5. Generate video
6. Download MP4

---

### Option 3: DIY with Free Tools
**Best for**: Budget-friendly, full control

**What you need:**
- Smartphone or camera
- Free editing software
- Stock footage (optional)
- 2-4 hours of time

**Step-by-step:**

#### Step 1: Gather Content
- [ ] Film your facility (wide shots, close-ups)
- [ ] Film your products (chippings, stones)
- [ ] Take photos of completed projects
- [ ] Get your logo file (PNG with transparent background)
- [ ] Download free stock footage (if needed)

#### Step 2: Choose Editing Software

**Free Desktop Options:**
- **DaVinci Resolve** (Best for quality)
  - Download: blackmagicdesign.com
  - Professional features
  - Learning curve: Medium

- **Shotcut** (Easiest to learn)
  - Download: shotcut.org
  - Simple interface
  - Learning curve: Easy

- **CapCut** (Mobile/Desktop)
  - Download: capcut.com
  - Very user-friendly
  - Learning curve: Easy

#### Step 3: Edit Your Video

**Basic Editing Steps:**
1. Import all footage/images
2. Arrange clips in timeline (follow script)
3. Add text overlays (company name, taglines)
4. Add background music
5. Add transitions between clips
6. Add your logo
7. Export as MP4

**Tutorial Resources:**
- YouTube: "How to edit promotional video [Software Name]"
- Search: "[Software Name] tutorial for beginners"

#### Step 4: Export Settings
- Format: MP4
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- Frame rate: 30 fps
- Quality: High
- File size: Keep under 20MB (may need compression)

#### Step 5: Compress Video
- Use HandBrake (free): handbrake.fr
- Or online: cloudconvert.com
- Target: Under 20MB while maintaining quality

---

### Option 4: Use Canva (Easiest)
**Best for**: No video editing experience needed

**Steps:**
1. Go to canva.com
2. Create account (free or pro)
3. Search: "promotional video template"
4. Choose construction/business template
5. Replace images with your photos
6. Add your text (from script)
7. Add your logo
8. Add background music (Canva library)
9. Export as MP4 video
10. Download

**Cost**: Free (with watermark) or $12.99/month (no watermark)
