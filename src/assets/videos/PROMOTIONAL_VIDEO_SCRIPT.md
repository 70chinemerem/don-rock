## Text Overlay Specifications

### Font Style
- **Primary**: Bold, Sans-serif (Arial, Helvetica, or similar)
- **Secondary**: Medium weight for details
- **Color**: White with dark shadow OR Blue (#2563eb) with white outline

### Text Timing
- Each text overlay: 3-5 seconds on screen
- Fade in: 0.5 seconds
- Fade out: 0.5 seconds
- Don't overcrowd - max 2 text elements at once

## Music Recommendations

### Style
- **Genre**: Corporate, Upbeat, Professional
- **Tempo**: Moderate to fast (100-120 BPM)
- **Mood**: Confident, Trustworthy, Energetic
- **Instruments**: Modern, not too orchestral

### Free Music Sources

2. **Bensound**

### Music Timing

## Voiceover Guidelines

### Voice Characteristics
- **Tone**: Professional, confident, friendly
- **Pace**: Clear, not rushed (150-160 words per minute)
- **Accent**: Nigerian English (if targeting local market)
- **Gender**: Either male or female, professional voice

### Script Word Count
- **60-second version**: ~150 words
- **30-second version**: ~75 words

### Recording Tips
- Use quality microphone
- Record in quiet environment
- Speak clearly and confidently
- Add slight pauses for emphasis
- Can be done with smartphone in quiet room

## Quick Production Tips

### If Using Smartphone
1. Film in landscape (horizontal)
2. Use 1080p or 4K resolution
3. Keep phone stable (use tripod)
4. Good lighting is essential
5. Record in quiet environment

### If Using Stock Footage
1. Search: "construction aggregates", "crushed stone", "road construction"
2. Use Pexels, Pixabay (free)
3. Mix with your own product shots
4. Ensure consistent style

### Editing Software
- **Free**: CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, Shotcut
- **Online**: Canva, Clipchamp
- **Mobile**: InShot, CapCut

### Export Settings
- Format: MP4
- Codec: H.264
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (or 1280x720)
- Frame rate: 24-30 fps
- Bitrate: 5-10 Mbps
- File size: Under 20MB
