# Promotional Ad Video Guide for DonRock Global Services

## Overview

This guide will help you create a professional promotional ad video for your DonRock Global Services website. The ad video section is prominently displayed on your homepage to showcase your business.

## Video Location

**File Path**: `src/assets/videos/promotional-ad.mp4`

The video will automatically appear in the "Promotional Ad Video Section" on your homepage once you add the file.

## Recommended Video Specifications

### Technical Requirements
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec) - Best compatibility
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080px (Full HD) or 1280x720px (HD)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (widescreen)
- **Duration**: 30-90 seconds (optimal for web)
- **File Size**: Under 20MB for fast loading
- **Frame Rate**: 24-30 fps
- **Audio**: Include background music and/or voiceover

### Video Content Suggestions

#### Opening (0-10 seconds)
- **Hook**: Start with an engaging visual
  - Aerial shot of construction site
  - Close-up of quality chippings/stones
  - Your company facility
- **Text Overlay**: "DonRock Global Services"
- **Tagline**: "Premium Construction Aggregates"

#### Main Content (10-60 seconds)
1. **Company Introduction** (10-20s)
   - Show your facility/operations
   - Display your logo
   - Highlight years of experience

2. **Products Showcase** (20-40s)
   - Granite chippings in various sizes
   - Crushed stones
   - Road base materials
   - Close-ups showing quality and texture
   - Products being used in construction

3. **Quality & Trust** (40-60s)
   - Quality control processes
   - Certifications (if available)
   - Happy clients/testimonials
   - Completed projects using your materials

#### Closing (60-90 seconds)
- **Call to Action**
  - Contact information
  - "Get a Quote Today"
  - Website/phone number
- **Logo & Tagline**
  - Final brand display
  - "DonRock Global Services - Building Nigeria's Future"

## Video Production Options

### Option 1: Professional Videography (Recommended)
**Best for**: High-quality, professional results

**What to include:**
- Hire a professional videographer
- Film at your facility
- Show products and operations
- Include voiceover narration
- Professional editing and graphics

**Cost**: Varies (typically ₦50,000 - ₦200,000+)

### Option 2: DIY with Smartphone
**Best for**: Budget-friendly option

**Tips:**
- Use a modern smartphone (iPhone/Android)
- Film in landscape mode (horizontal)
- Ensure good lighting
- Use a tripod or stable surface
- Record in 1080p or 4K
- Edit with free apps (CapCut, InShot, DaVinci Resolve)

**Free Editing Software:**
- **CapCut** (Mobile/Desktop) - Easy to use
- **DaVinci Resolve** (Desktop) - Professional features
- **Shotcut** (Desktop) - Open source
- **Canva** (Online) - Simple video editing

### Option 3: Stock Video + Your Content
**Best for**: Quick production with professional footage

**Resources:**
- **Pexels Videos** (pexels.com/videos) - Free stock videos
- **Pixabay Videos** (pixabay.com/videos) - Free stock videos
- Mix stock footage with your own product shots
- Add your logo and text overlays

## Video Optimization

### Before Uploading
1. **Compress Video**:
   - Use HandBrake (free)
   - Target: Under 20MB
   - Maintain 1080p quality

2. **Test Playback**:
   - Check on different devices
   - Ensure audio is clear
   - Verify text is readable

3. **Create Thumbnail**:
   - Use an engaging frame from video
   - Add text: "Watch Our Story"
   - Save as poster image

## Adding the Video to Your Website

### Step 1: Create/Obtain Video
Follow the guidelines above to create your promotional video.

### Step 2: Optimize Video
- Compress to under 20MB
- Ensure MP4 format (H.264)
- 16:9 aspect ratio

### Step 3: Add to Project
1. Place video file in: `src/assets/videos/promotional-ad.mp4`
2. The video will automatically appear on your website

### Step 4: Add Poster Image (Optional)
- Create a thumbnail/poster image
- Place in: `src/assets/images/promotional-ad-poster.jpeg`
- Update poster attribute in HTML if needed

## Tips for Success

1. **Keep it Short**: 30-60 seconds is optimal for web
2. **Show, Don't Tell**: Use visuals over text when possible
3. **Professional Quality**: Even DIY videos should look polished
4. **Clear Message**: What makes DonRock different?
5. **Strong Opening**: First 5 seconds are crucial
6. **Clear CTA**: Make it easy for viewers to take action
7. **Mobile First**: Most viewers will watch on phones
8. **Test Everything**: Watch on different devices before publishing
