# Video Requirements for DonRock Global Services

## Suggested Video Content

### 1. Hero Background Video (Optional Enhancement)
- **File**: `hero-background.mp4`
- **Purpose**: Replace or complement the hero section background
- **Content**: Construction site, aggregates being processed, or facility operations
- **Specs**: 1920x1080px, 10-30 seconds, looped, muted, autoplay
- **Size**: Under 5MB

### 2. Product Demonstration Videos
- **Files**: 
  - `granite-chippings-demo.mp4`
  - `crushed-stones-demo.mp4`
  - `road-base-demo.mp4`
- **Purpose**: Show products in action or close-up quality shots
- **Content**: Product close-ups, texture details, size variations
- **Specs**: 1280x720px, 30-60 seconds
- **Size**: Under 10MB each

### 3. Facility Tour Video
- **File**: `facility-tour.mp4`
- **Purpose**: Show company operations, processing plant, quality control
- **Content**: Walkthrough of facility, equipment, operations
- **Specs**: 1920x1080px, 1-3 minutes
- **Size**: Under 15MB

### 4. Testimonial Videos (Optional)
- **Files**: `testimonial-[client-name].mp4`
- **Purpose**: Client testimonials and reviews
- **Content**: Client interviews, project showcases
- **Specs**: 1280x720px, 1-2 minutes
- **Size**: Under 15MB each

### 5. Process/How It Works Video
- **File**: `production-process.mp4`
- **Purpose**: Explain your production process and quality control
- **Content**: From raw materials to finished product
- **Specs**: 1920x1080px, 2-3 minutes
- **Size**: Under 20MB

## Video Production Tips

### Content Ideas
- Show your products being used in real construction projects
- Demonstrate quality through close-up shots
- Feature your team and operations
- Showcase completed projects using your materials
- Explain your quality control process

### Technical Requirements
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec) - Primary format
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080px (Full HD) or 1280x720px (HD)
- **Frame Rate**: 24-30 fps
- **Audio**: Include audio for informational videos, mute for background videos
- **Subtitles**: Consider adding subtitles for accessibility

### File Size Guidelines
- Background videos: Under 5MB
- Product demos: Under 10MB
- Feature videos: Under 15MB
- Long-form content: Under 20MB

## Where to Get Videos

### Option 1: Create Your Own (Recommended)
- Use smartphone or camera to record
- Focus on product quality and operations
- Good lighting and stable shots
- Edit with free tools like DaVinci Resolve, Shotcut, or online editors

### Option 2: Professional Videography
- Hire a videographer for high-quality content
- Best for marketing and brand building
- Can create multiple videos in one session

### Option 3: Stock Video (If Needed)
- **Pexels Videos** (pexels.com/videos)
- **Pixabay Videos** (pixabay.com/videos)
- Search: "construction aggregates", "crushed stone", "road construction"

## Video Optimization Tools

### Free Tools
- **HandBrake**: Video compression and format conversion
- **FFmpeg**: Command-line video processing
- **CloudConvert**: Online video conversion
- **Clideo**: Online video compressor

### Compression Settings
- Codec: H.264
- Bitrate: 2-5 Mbps for web
- Resolution: Match display size (don't exceed 1920x1080)
- Frame Rate: 24-30 fps

## Implementation Notes

1. Videos are automatically handled by Vite
2. Use relative paths: `src/assets/videos/filename.mp4`
3. Always include poster images for better UX
4. Consider mobile users - keep file sizes reasonable
5. Test on different devices and browsers
