## Image Guidelines

### Format
- **File Format**: JPG or JPEG (preferred for photos)
- **File Size**: Keep under 500KB each for fast loading
- **Quality**: High resolution (at least 2x display size for retina screens)

### Dimensions
- **Hero Background**: 1920x1080px (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **Product Images**: 800x600px (4:3 aspect ratio)
- **Company Facility**: 1200x800px (3:2 aspect ratio)

### Content Suggestions

#### For Product Images:
- Take close-up photos of your actual products
- Show texture and quality clearly
- Use good lighting
- Keep background simple (white or neutral)

#### For Hero Background:
- Construction site with aggregates
- Pile of chippings/stones
- Industrial/construction scene
- Should convey professionalism and quality

#### For Company Facility:
- Your processing plant
- Storage area with product piles
- Equipment in operation
- Company building/facility

## Where to Get Images

### Option 1: Take Your Own Photos (Recommended)
- Use a smartphone or camera
- Ensure good lighting
- Focus on product quality and detail

### Option 2: Free Stock Photo Sites
- **Unsplash** (unsplash.com) - Search: "construction aggregates", "crushed stone", "road base"
- **Pexels** (pexels.com) - Search: "construction materials", "gravel", "aggregates"
- **Pixabay** (pixabay.com) - Search: "construction stones", "chippings"

### Option 3: Professional Photography
- Hire a photographer for product shots
- Best for marketing materials

## File Naming

**IMPORTANT**: Use these exact filenames (case-sensitive):
- `hero-background.jpg`
- `company-facility.jpg`
- `road-base-materials.jpg`
- `fine-chippings.jpg`
- `coarse-aggregates.jpg`

## After Adding Images

1. Place files in: `src/assets/images/`
2. Ensure filenames match exactly
3. Images will automatically appear on the website
4. The site has fallback gradients if images are missing

## Current Placeholders

The website currently uses SVG placeholders for missing images. Once you add the actual JPG files, they will automatically replace the placeholders.
